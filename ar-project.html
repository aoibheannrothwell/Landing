DeliverEquality

Deliver Equality is a non-profit organisation which exists to help supply internet access to disadvantaged students from DEIS schools.

COVID-19 has caused a major threat to progress on educational disadvantage as all schools are forced from classroom to online learning. There is a digital divide between students from DEIS and non-DEIS schools, meaning there is a huge concern that this pandemic will exacerbate already existing educational inequalities.

Together we can help bridge this digital divide between DEIS and non-DEIS students during COVID-19
